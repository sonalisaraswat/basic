In-Place Operators--
>>>x=x+3   can also be written as
>>>x+=3
---------------------------------------
>>>x=x*3   can be written as
>>>x*=3
---------------------------------------
>>>x=x%3
>>>x%=3
---------------------------------------
>>>x=x-3
>>>x-=3
---------------------------------------
>>>x=x/3
>>>x/=3
---------------------------------------
Python does not support the increment and decrement operators(++ or --).Thus (x++) cannot be used for (x=x+1).
